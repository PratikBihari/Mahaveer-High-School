Our Profile

Mahaveer High School, Barimundei

Established in 1992, our school is managed by the Pvt. Aided sector. Located in the rural landscape of Niali Block, Cuttack, we specialize in high school education (Grades 8 to 10).

All-weather road approach
No attached pre-primary section
Non-shift school nature
Private building ownership

School Infrastructure

Classroom Details

The school has 1 instructional classroom in good condition. Additionally, there are 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities.

Admin Space

A separate room is provided for the Head master/Teacher for administrative and instructional management.

Drinking Water

Functional Hand Pumps are the primary source of drinking water. The school has 1 boys and 1 girls toilet, both fully functional.

Library & Sports

A functional library with a collection of 145 books. The school features a dedicated playground for students.

Mid-Day Meal

Nutritious Mid-day meals are prepared and provided directly in the school premises.

Technology & Access

The school has electric connection. Currently, no computers or CAL labs are available for teaching. Ramp access is not required.
